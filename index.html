Minecraft Java Edition 1.7.X to 1.17.X

And even third-party clients.

We currently offer Bedwars.
Other mini-games are coming soon.

The Trix Network offers two whitelist-enabled SMPs alternatively: Derpitcraft and The Modified.

Derpitcraft is a survival economy server, with a vanilla-like experience. It has strong anti-grief measures and regular backups. We saw over 200 registrations for season 7.
Season 8 will launch after the release of 1.18.

The Modified is a modded SMP that stays close to the roots of vanilla. The second season saw over 50 registrations. It has the same protection as Derpitcraft.
Season 3 will launch after Derpitcraft season 8 ends.
