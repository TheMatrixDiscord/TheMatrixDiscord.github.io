Our History

The Matrix Network was formed from our discord server, where we used to have a Hermitcraft-style SMP called DerpitCraft.. But since our members wanted more gamemodes we made The Matrix Network. The whole network is loved by our community, the server is well-moderated by our staff and has very good anticheat to keep cheaters out.. Our staff is very friendly and is always ready to help. All you need to do is raise a ticket and rest assured your problem will be solved.
