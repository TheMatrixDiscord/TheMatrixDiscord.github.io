DerpitCraft

DerpitCraft is a vanilla-like survival economy SMP, on both Java and Bedrock, with cracked-support!
We have a friendly community, advanced moderation and anti-grief system, and a whitelist to ensure you have the best collaborative Minecraft experience!
With over 100 registrations in Season 7, the coming seasons are sure to be amazing!
